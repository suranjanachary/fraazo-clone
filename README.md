
  
  Fraazo is an online food and grocery shopping platform, where the users can buy fresh fruits, vegetables, oats, and kitchen staples directly from the ease of their home.
 
 # Features
 
 - Login / Sign up
 - Best deals
 - Product details page
 - Place orders
